Command Detail

Claims

/abandonclaim

Abandons a claim

/abandonallclaims

Abandons ALL your claims

/abandontopclaim

Abandons a claim and all its subdivisions

/basicclaims

Aliases: bc

Switches the shovel tool back to basic claims mode.

/claim

Claims a selection made in WorldEdit. Note: This command will not work unless the server has WorldEdit.

/claimbook [player]

Gives a player a manual about claiming land.

/claimfarewell <"message">

Sets the farewell message of your claim

To unset, /claimfarewell ""

/claimgreeting <"message">

Sets the greeting message of your claim

To unset, /claimgreeting ""

/claimbuy

View a list of claims for sale. Click [Buy] to purchase.

/claimsell [price]

Puts your claim up for sale at the set price. To disable sale, set the price to -1 or set ForSale setting in /claiminfo to false.

/buyclaimblocks [numberOfBlocks]

Aliases: buyclaim

Purchases additional claim blocks with server money. Doesn't work on servers without any economy plugin.

/sellclaimblocks [numberOfBlocks]

Aliases: sellclaim

Sell your claim blocks for server money. Doesn't work on servers without any economy plugin.

/cuboid

Aliases: cuboidclaims

Toggles 3D cuboid claims mode.

/inherit

Aliases: inheritpermissions

Toggles subdivision inherit mode

/claimlist [<player> [world]]

Aliases: claimslist

List information about a player's claim blocks and claims.

/claiminfo [id]

Aliases: claimsinfo

Gets information about a claim you are standing in or by claim id.

/claimsetspawn

Aliases: claimsetspawn

Sets the spawn of your claim to the location you are standing in.

/claimspawn

Aliases: claimspawn

Teleports you to claim spawn, if available.

/claimsubdivide

Aliases: subdivideclaims, sc

Switches the shovel tool to subdivision mode, used to subdivide your claims

/claimtransfer [player]

Aliases: transferclaim

Transfer the claim you're standing in to a player.

/givepet cancel|<player>

Allows a player to give away a pet they tamed.

/claimname ["name"]

Sets the name of your claim

/playerinfo <player> <world>|<player>|[<world>]

Gets information about a player

Flags

/claimflagdebug

Aliases: cfd

Toggles claim flag debug mode

/claimflag [<flag> <source> <target> <value> [context] | <target> <value> [context]]

Aliases: cf

Gets/Sets claim flags in the claim you are standing in.

/claimflaggroup <group> <flag> <source> <target> <value> | <target> <value>

Aliases: cfg

Gets/Sets flag permission for a group in claim you are standing in.

/claimflagplayer <player> <flag> <source> <target> <value> | <target> <value>

Aliases: cfp

Adds flag permission to player.

/claimflagreset

Aliases: cfr

Resets a claim to flag defaults.

Options

/claimoption [<option> <value>]

Aliases: co

Gets/Sets options in the claim you are standing in.

/claimoptiongroup <group> [<option> <value>]

Aliases: cog

Gets/Sets options for a group in claim you are standing in.

/claimoptionplayer <player> [<option> <value>]

Aliases: cop

Gets/Sets options for a player in claim you are standing in.

Trust

/accesstrust <player> <group>

Aliases: at

Grants a player entry to your claim(s) and use of your bed

/permissiontrust <player>|<group>

Aliases: pt

Grants a player permission to grant their level of permission to others

/containertrust <player>|<group>

Aliases: ct

Grants a player access to your claim's containers, crops, animals, bed, buttons, and levers

/trust <player>|<group>

Aliases: t

Grants a player edit access to your claim(s)

/trustall <player>|<group>

Aliases: ta

Grants player's access to all your claims

/untrust <player>|<group>

Aliases: ut

Revokes a player's access to your claim

/untrustall <player>|<group>

Aliases: uta

Revokes a player's access to all your claims

/trustlist

Lists permissions for the claim you're standing in

Admin

/adjustbonusclaimblocks <player> <amount> [world]

Aliases: acb

Adds or subtracts bonus claim blocks for a player

/setaccruedclaimblocks <player> <amount> [<world>]

Aliases: scb

Updates a player's accrued claim block total.

/adminclaims

Aliases: ac

Switches the shovel tool to administrative claims mode

/adminclaimlist [world]

Aliases: adminclaimslist, claimadminlist

List all administrative claims

/deleteclaim

Aliases: dc

Deletes the claim you're standing in, even if it's not your claim

/deleteallclaims [player]

Aliases: dac

Delete all of another player's claims

/deletealladminclaims

Deletes all administrative claims

/ignoreclaims

Aliases: ic

Toggles ignore claims mode

/claimclear <target> [<claim> [<world>]]

Allows clearing of entities within one or more claims.

/claimpermissiongroup <group> [<permission> <value>]

Aliases: cpg

Sets a permission on a group with a claim context

/claimpermissionplayer <player> [<permission> <value>]

Aliases: cpp

Sets a permission on a player with a claim context

/restorenature

Aliases: rn

Switches the shovel tool to restoration mode

/restorenatureaggressive

Aliases: rna

Switches the shovel tool to aggressive restoration mode

/restorenaturefill [radius]

Aliases: rnf

Switches the shovel tool to fill mode

/unlockdrops

Allows other players to pick up the items you dropped when you died

/gpdebug <on>|<off>|<record>|<paste> [<player>]

Toggles debug

/gpreload

Reloads Grief Prevention's configuration settings

Chat

/ignoredplayerlist

Aliases: ignoredlist

Lists the players you're ignoring in chat

/ignoreplayer [player]

Aliases: ignore

Ignores another player's chat messages

/unignoreplayer [player]

Aliases: unignore

Unignores another player's chat messages

/separate <player1> <player2>

Forces two players to ignore each other in chat

/unseparate <player1> <player2>

Reverses /separate

/softmute <player>

Toggles whether a player's messages will only reach other soft-muted players

Misc

/gphelp

Lists detailed information on each command.
